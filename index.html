Why do I need to Maintain a tasklist ??    



To be well organized in the workplace, you need to be using Task Lists. By using them, you will ensure that:
1. You remember to carry out all necessary tasks.
2. You tackle the most important jobs first, and don't waste time on trivial tasks.
3. You don't get stressed by a large number of unimportant jobs.
4. Start by listing all of the tasks that you must carry out.


Mark the importance of the task next to it, with a priority from A (very important) to F (unimportant).
Redraft the list into this order of importance. Then carry out the jobs at the top of the list first. These are the most important, most beneficial tasks to complete.
You can also use software-based approaches to manage your list.
You can often access these from anywhere, and they can often be synced with your Smartphone or PDA.
